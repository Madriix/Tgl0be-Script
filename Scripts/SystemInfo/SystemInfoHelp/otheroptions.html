Other Options

Here is an explaination of what some of the other options do in SystemInfo by C}{$.

Respond to CTCP version with your /stats <----- Does what it says :D

Remove all formatting from all outputs <----- Removes all formatting from ALL output.

Delay between /info lines <----- The amount of time between each line in /info to avoid flooding etc.

Limit /space output to 5 disk <---- For People who have many partitions.
